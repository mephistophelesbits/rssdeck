## The Real Skill: Clear Thinking

## My Practical Tips for "Vibe Coders"

1. **Start with the vibe, not the code**
   - Describe the user experience first
   - Let AI figure out the implementation

2. **Use the right tools**
   - Claude, ChatGPT, Antigravity
   - Each has different strengths

3. **Review like a product manager, not a compiler**
   - "Does this feel right?"
   - "Is this what I imagined?"
   - Not "Is this syntax correct?"

4. **Embrace iteration**
   - First version won't be perfect
   - Each round gets closer

5. **Document your vision**
   - Write down what you want before starting
   - Helps you communicate with AI
